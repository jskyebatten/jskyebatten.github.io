Research Vision

My research investigates how educational contexts — including learning technologies, pedagogical practices, and institutional policies — shape student engagement and persistence in computing education. I am newly interested in the emerging questions of how AI integration in higher education affects student learning outcomes, with an emphasis on practices that support all students' success.

Thread 01 — Adoption

Investigating how computing faculty and students adopt and utilize AI tools across diverse institutional contexts.

Thread 02 — Impacts

Examining how AI affects student engagement, learning outcomes, and persistence across different student populations.

Thread 03 — Resources

Translating findings into evidence-based frameworks and guidelines for computing educators.

Dissertation: The Role of Mindset Messages in Broadening Participation in Computing: An Ethnographic Study on Student Engagement — Advisor: Dr. Monique Ross
